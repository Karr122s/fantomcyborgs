FAQ

What is Fantom Cyborgs?
Fantom Cyborgs is a collection of 4000 NFT's that will randomly reward
its minter/hodler some FTM.

Why Fantom Network?
Fantom Network has a lower gas fee which makes a pretty
attractive blockchain for a project that we built.

How much is the minting price of a Fantom Cyborg?
Each Fantom Cyborg will be for sale for only 5FTM. Why 5FTM?
We will use 20% of each sale will be rewarded to a random Cyborg holder/minter.

Why only use 20% of each sale?
This is to reserve a percentage of the raised funds to be donated to #TeamSeas.

Is there any bigger reward for hodlers?
When the entire collection is fully minted, there will be a Big gift for a random hodler/minter.

How many Fantom Cyborgs will be in circulation?
There will only be 4000 maximum supply of Fantom Cyborgs NFT's.

Is there any whitelisting?
No. You might ask why? We prefer that there will be no whitelisting, the minting should be open for all
and not just for whitelisted addresses. This gives a sense to the whole "decentralized" concept.
